Welcome To Iqra Educational Institute
“Iqra” the First Commandment from Allah to his beloved Messenger “Muhammad (SAW)” is an order from the Almighty “to read”; the name of the institution was unconsciously taken up as Iqra. With the passage of time, we felt the mass of the task which undoubtedly gains inertia with time, we feel weaker and weaker to lift it, Had Allah not shown his Grandeur and Gratitude that blessed us to carry on. The school had a humble beginning of a few rooms for a few classes scattered in a few residential rooms of the inmates, the rooms that somehow exceeded their basic needs. The residents had just migrated to this new settlement of Bemina from varied corners and a few of them from far-flung pockets of the province of Kashmir. Why Choose Iqra Educational Institute?

Iqra Educational Institute is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering an inclusive environment for holistic development. Here’s why we stand out.

Quality Education

Our institute ensures high-quality education with experienced teachers committed to the growth and development of every student.

Safe Environment

We prioritize safety with CCTV monitoring and security staff to maintain a secure and inclusive environment for all.

Holistic Development

Focusing on mind, body, and character, we shape well-rounded individuals equipped for future challenges.

Modern Facilities

Our institute offers state-of-the-art facilities, including smart classrooms and updated technology for enhanced learning.

Engaged Community

We encourage active parent and community involvement, fostering a collaborative and supportive educational journey.

Strong Values

Our curriculum includes values education, teaching respect, kindness, and responsibility to nurture well-rounded citizens.
